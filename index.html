01 — My story

I'm a fullstack engineer who took the scenic route.

After nearly a decade in nonprofits and education, I got tired of broken systems and taught myself how to build better ones. Today I build across the stack, shipping products in gaming and e-commerce and working on small teams where I can own a feature from idea to production.
